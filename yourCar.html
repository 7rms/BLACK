Renter Requirements

  1. Age: Must be at least 21 years old.
  2. Valid Documents: A valid driving license (Saudi or recognized international license) and proof of identity (National ID or Passport).
  3. Credit Card: May be required for payment and security deposit, with sufficient available balance.
  4. Criminal Record: Must be free of criminal convictions.

Renter Obligations

  1. Vehicle Condition: Receive the vehicle in good condition and take proper care of it.
  2. Usage: Use the vehicle for intended purposes only; not allowed for racing or transporting unauthorized goods.
  3. Modifications: No modifications or additions to the vehicle without the company’s approval.
  4. Insurance: Comply with the terms of the insurance policy.
  5. Delays: Late returns may incur a fee equivalent to the daily rental rate.
  6. Fuel: Return the vehicle with the same fuel level as received or pay refueling charges.
  7. Damages and Violations: Responsible for any traffic violations, damages, or car defects, including those not covered by insurance.
